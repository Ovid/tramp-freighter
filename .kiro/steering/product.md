# Product Overview

Tramp Freighter Blues - A single-player space trading survival game built on the Sol Sector Starmap foundation.

## Purpose

Create a character-driven space trading game where players navigate between star systems, engage in commodity trading, manage ship resources, and build relationships with NPCs across the sector. The game emphasizes financial tension, meaningful choices, and the feeling of being part of distinct human communities scattered across the stars.

## Foundation

Built on top of the Sol Sector Starmap Visualization (v1.1) - an interactive 3D star map of 117 star systems within 20 light-years of Sol, connected by wormhole networks.

## Key Features

- Navigate between wormhole-connected star systems
- Buy low, sell high commodity trading with dynamic prices
- Ship resource management (fuel, hull, engine, life support)
- Relationship system with memorable NPCs
- Event-driven narrative with meaningful choices
- Financial pressure through debt and recurring costs
- Ship upgrades with tradeoffs
- Save/load system with auto-save

## Design Pillars

- **"You Know These People"**: Every station feels inhabited by memorable individuals
- **"Every Credit Counts"**: Financial pressure creates meaningful decisions
- **"Your Ship, Your Story"**: The ship has character, quirks, and personality
- **"The Universe Doesn't Wait"**: Time passes, prices change, NPCs pursue their own goals

## Target Users

- Players who enjoy trading/economic simulation games
- Fans of character-driven narratives in space settings
- Players who appreciate meaningful choices and resource management
